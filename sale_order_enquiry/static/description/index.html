Overview

    Overview

  • Order Enquiry System: This is a feature that allows users to create and manage inquiries about orders. Think of it as a way to track and manage potential sales before they are finalized.
  • Enquiry Management: Users can create, view, and manage order inquiries, including details like the customer, products, and quantities.
  • Order Lines: Each inquiry can have multiple lines detailing the products and their quantities.
  • Sale Orders Integration: Convert inquiries into sale orders and manage them directly.

    Benefits

  • Centralized Info: All details related to a potential order, such as the customer, products, and pricing, are organized in one place. This makes it easier to track and manage inquiries.
  • Easy Addition: Quickly add multiple product lines to sales orders or inquiries without manually entering each line item, saving time and reducing errors.
  • Multiple Options: Choose how to add product lines based on various sources like specific sales orders, customer orders, or order inquiries. Multi-Order Handling: Add products to multiple sales orders in one go, streamlining the process for large or complex orders.
  • Flexible Settings: Control whether to clear existing lines before adding new ones or to add lines alongside existing ones. This ensures you can tailor the tool to your specific needs.
  • Streamlined Process: Instead of manually handling inquiries, this system automates creating sale orders from inquiries, saving time and reducing errors.
  • Automatic Updates: The system automatically calculates the total amounts and taxes based on the products and quantities in the inquiry.
  • Better Service: Sales teams can quickly see the details of each inquiry, leading to faster responses and better customer service.
  • Multi-Order Handling: Users can handle multiple sales orders at once from a single inquiry, which is useful for large or complex orders.

    Usage

  • Start New Inquiry: Users can create a new order inquiry, filling in details like the customer, date, and products they are interested in.
  • Add Products: Add products to the inquiry with their prices and quantities. This helps in calculating the total amount and taxes automatically.
  • Confirm Inquiry: Once all details are added, users can confirm the inquiry, which then creates a sale order.
  • View Sale Orders: Users can view the sale orders generated from the inquiry to track their progress.
  • Add Products: Users can add products to an inquiry directly from sales, making it easier to handle new or additional products.
  • Using the Sales Line Wizard:
    • Select Criteria: Choose whether to add lines based on a specific sales order, a customer, or an order inquiry.
    • Add Lines: The wizard will retrieve the relevant product lines and add them to your selected sales order or inquiry. You can also handle multiple orders at once if needed.
  • Using the Product Add Wizard:
    • Choose Order Type: Decide if you want to add products to a single sales order or multiple orders.
    • Set Product Details: Enter product details like price, quantity, and taxes.
    • Add Product: The wizard will create the necessary product lines in the selected sales orders, based on the details you provided.
